# 🧭 About AIStarter

AIStarter is a forward‑looking AI management platform.
It helps individuals and teams quickly deploy, manage, and use AI tools, models, plugins, and workflows without complex environment setup.

- **Desktop Edition**
  Designed for creators and developers. Focuses on efficiency and ease of use to enable local AI workflows.

- **Server Edition**
  Targeted at teams and enterprises. Supports multi‑user management, remote job scheduling, and a unified AI service panel (in development).
  Provides centralized operations and management for studios, companies, and AI service providers.

> "The Desktop Edition empowers individuals; the Server Edition empowers teams."

## ⚙️ Key Features

- 🧩 One‑click launch for mainstream AI apps (Ollama, ComfyUI, SD WebUI, RVC, etc.)
- 🪶 Virtual environment isolation to avoid dependency conflicts; portable across machines
- ⚡ Automatic GPU/CPU detection and switching
- 🧠 Model auto‑management and categorized loading
- 🧰 Built‑in marketplace for plugins and workflows
- 🌐 Offline support and local model invocation
- 🪄 Project composition, automated workflows, and custom nodes
- 🖥️ Cross‑platform support (Windows / macOS / Linux)
- 🔒 Privacy by design: all tasks run locally, nothing uploaded to the cloud
